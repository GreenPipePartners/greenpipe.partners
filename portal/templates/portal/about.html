The mission

Open software that reaches the work.

Green Pipe Partners builds and champions open-source software for manufacturing. We believe the strongest foundation is one that engineers can inspect, understand, adapt, and carry forward themselves.

Source code alone is not enough. We configure the workbench, train the people using it, and demonstrate how the same open foundation can support controlled modernization in the plant.

How the work fits together

One foundation, from engineering capability to panel modernization.

01 / AgentLab

Build capability first.

AgentLab is the configured open-source workbench and training foundation. It gives controls teams a reproducible environment for learning, development, and authorized engineering workflows.

02 / PanelLock

Apply the same workbench.

PanelLock is the future-facing proof and application of AgentLab for controlled panel modernization. The work stays grounded in the same configured environment rather than becoming a separate product stack.

Keep the foundation open.
