Flux is Green Pipe's controls workbench for industrial and manufacturing development.

## What it is for

- Operator-first SCADA and controls interfaces.
- Repeatable screen, tag, alarm, and reporting patterns.
- Practical data paths between field equipment, Ignition, Python, and historians.
- Durable project structure for oil & gas, aerospace, and water/wastewater work.
- ESXi-compatible deployment environments behind a Flux gateway.
- Time-series data consolidation for faster controls workflows.
- LLM subscription management for controls platform users.
- Fluxup-based install with `sudo "$(command -v uvx)" fluxup init`.

## Design goals

- Keep operators close to the process.
- Reduce wait time and brittle one-off tooling.
- Make controls work easier to review, repeat, and maintain.
